Creator Manager Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Creator Manager handles information when authorised users connect and analyse supported social media accounts.

1. Information We May Collect

Depending on permissions granted by the user, Creator Manager may process:

  • Social account username, display name and profile information.
  • Follower, following, likes and public video counts.
  • Public content information and performance metrics.
  • Views, likes, comments, shares and other available analytics.
  • Connection status, synchronisation time and technical error logs.

2. How Information Is Used

Information may be used to:

  • Display account and content analytics.
  • Measure follower growth and posting consistency.
  • Calculate account-health indicators.
  • Identify performance trends.
  • Maintain and troubleshoot account connections.

3. Permissions and Authorisation

Creator Manager only accesses supported platform data after the account owner or authorised manager grants the requested permissions.

4. Access Tokens

Platform access tokens may be processed by a secure backend to maintain authorised account connections. Tokens are not intended to be displayed publicly or stored directly within public application code.

5. Data Sharing

Creator Manager does not sell personal information. Information may be processed by infrastructure and social platform providers where necessary to operate the application.

6. Data Retention

Analytics and account snapshots may be retained to provide historical comparisons. Information may be removed when it is no longer required or when an authorised account is disconnected.

7. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organisational measures are used to protect connected-account information. However, no electronic system can guarantee absolute security.

8. Disconnecting an Account

An authorised user may disconnect a social account. Disconnecting may stop future synchronisation and revoke or invalidate the associated platform authorisation where supported.

9. Third-Party Services

TikTok, Instagram, Supabase and other integrated services process information under their own privacy policies and platform requirements.

10. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as Creator Manager develops or platform requirements change. The latest version will remain available on this page.

11. Contact

Privacy-related questions may be directed to the authorised administrator of Creator Manager.
